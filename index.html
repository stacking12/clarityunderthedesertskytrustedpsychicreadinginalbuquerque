What to Know Before Your Visit

What is a psychic reading and how does it work?

A psychic reading is a one-on-one session where your reader draws on intuition, tarot, or astrology to explore the questions you bring — often around love, career, or a decision you're facing. You'll talk through what's on your mind, and your reader will share insight and perspective to help you see it more clearly.

How should I prepare for my first tarot card reading?

Come with an open mind and, if it helps, a specific question or area of your life you'd like to focus on. There's nothing special you need to bring — just yourself and whatever is on your mind.

Do you offer palm reading and astrology reading sessions?

Yes. Alongside psychic and tarot card readings, Psychic Albuquerque offers palm reading, astrology reading, aura reading, energy healing, and chakra balancing sessions.

How long does a session take, and what does it cost?

Session length and pricing vary depending on the type of reading and what you'd like to cover. Call 505-305-6855 and Psychic Albuquerque will walk you through the options and find a time that works for you.

Is my session confidential?

Yes. Every conversation at Psychic Albuquerque stays private. You're free to speak openly about whatever brought you in.
